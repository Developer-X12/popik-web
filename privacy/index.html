This Privacy Policy sets forth how user information is handled in Popik (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

The Service collects the following information:

1. Account / Authentication Information

  • Identifiers provided through Apple/Google login (user ID, etc.)
  • (Depending on settings) email address, etc.

In the case of guest use, login information may not be collected, or may be handled in a limited manner.

2. Content Provided by Users

  • Posted images (two images) and captions
  • Text such as comments
  • Post creation date/time and metadata associated with Posts (e.g., image format, size, etc.)

3. Usage Data

  • Behavioral data such as voting history and likes
  • Moderation-related data such as reports and blocks

4. Device / Log Information

  • IP address, user agent, access timestamps
  • Crash logs, error logs, etc. (for incident analysis)
  • OS/device information (overview)

5. Advertising-Related Information

  • Information necessary for ad delivery and measurement (ad ID, etc.)
  • Events such as ad impressions and clicks

These may be collected and processed primarily by advertising providers (e.g., Google AdMob).

2. Purposes of Use

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing the Service (posting, voting, commenting, etc.)
  • Authentication, identity verification, prevention of unauthorized use, and security
  • Responding to inquiries
  • Incident response, log analysis, quality improvement, and feature improvements
  • Displaying ads, measuring ad effectiveness, and preventing ad fraud
  • Responding to violations of the Terms (investigation, removal, suspension, etc.)

3. Use of External Services (Outsourcing / Third-Party Provision)

The Service uses external services to provide functionality. External service providers may process information in accordance with their own policies.

  • Supabase: authentication, database, etc.
  • Cloudflare (R2/Workers): image storage and delivery, etc.
  • Google AdMob: ad delivery and measurement, etc.

Within the scope of the purposes above, the Operator may outsource or provide necessary information to external services.

4. Advertising / Tracking

The Service may use ad IDs, etc. for ad delivery. Users may control this via device settings (e.g., limit ad tracking, limit personalized ads, etc.). Note that such settings may reduce ad relevance.

5. Retention Period

The Operator retains information for the period necessary for the purposes of use. If a User deletes a Post, it will in principle become hidden on the Service; however, it may be retained for a certain period for backups, logs, fraud prevention, legal compliance, etc.

6. User Rights (Disclosure / Deletion, etc.)

Users may request deletion of account information or Posts via the functions in the Service or by contacting us. Due to legal or operational reasons, immediate deletion may not be possible.

7. Security Measures

The Operator implements reasonable security measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of information. However, complete security is not guaranteed.

8. Use by Minors

The Service is intended for Users aged 13 and older. Persons under 13 may not use the Service. Minors should use the Service after obtaining the consent of a parent or guardian, etc., as necessary.
