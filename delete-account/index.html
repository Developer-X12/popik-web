Request Account Deletion

To delete your Popik account and all associated data (posts, votes, and profile information), please follow the instructions below.

How to delete:

1. Open the Popik app and go to your Profile.
2. Tap the Settings icon.
3. Select Delete Account and confirm.

If you no longer have the app installed, you can request deletion by emailing us from your registered email address:

Email Support to Delete

Data handled upon deletion:

  • Deleted: Profile info, all your Posts, and your Voting history.
  • Retention: Some logs may be retained for 30 days for security purposes before permanent deletion.
