Child Safety & CSAE Protection Standards

Popik is committed to providing a safe social environment. We have a zero-tolerance policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

1. Monitoring and Removal

We use automated tools and human reporting systems to monitor, detect, and instantly remove any content that violates child safety standards.

2. Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report inappropriate content or suspicious behavior directly through the in-app "Report" button. All child safety reports are prioritized and investigated within 24 hours.

3. Cooperation with Authorities

We comply with all legal requirements and will report illegal activities to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement agencies.
